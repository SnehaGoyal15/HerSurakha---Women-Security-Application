Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How does SOS work?
When you press the SOS button, all your emergency contacts receive an alert with your live Google Maps location.
❓ Is my data safe?
Yes! Your data is stored securely in Firebase. Only you can see your contacts.
❓ Can I use it without internet?
Internet is required for SOS alerts. But Fake Call works offline!
❓ How to add emergency contacts?
Go to Contacts → Add New Contact. Fill details and save.
